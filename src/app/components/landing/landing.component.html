It's Ability

Our project addresses the social challenge of reduced inequalities. We focus on the easing of the communication barrier between the deaf and healthcare officers in the medical sector. We plan on implementing a machine learning algorithm that will translate Kenyan Sign Language to text, and vice versa, thereby maximising the use of Information Technology to redesign communication in the healthcare sector for the deaf.
