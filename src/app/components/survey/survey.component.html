Are You a Medical Practitioner or a Deaf/Hard-of-hearing Individual?

As we commenced this ambitious project, we made it our goal to meet the needs of our key stakeholders, medical practitioners, and the Deaf/HoH individuals.

As a result, we generated a questionnaire to better understand the future potential users of our system.

We have also prepared a questionnaire for the general public, in order to gauge their awareness on the effects of reduced inequalities with regards to the Deaf/HoH.

We would love you to take a few minutes to answer these questions, so as to further increase the user experience.

NOTE: This survey is only for those residing within the country of Kenya.
